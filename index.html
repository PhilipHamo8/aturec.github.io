ATU Recreational Basketball League

 All campuses, all- inclusive, every week.

The ATU Recrational Basketball League is an all-inclusive league in which students and staff of the ATU sign up and are split into teams and play in recreational games weekly. At the end of each semester, a cross-campus tournament will take place in Sligo (due to it being the most central campus).

The main aim of this "league" is to keep people playing basketball throughout college, without the stresses of organised, competitive basketball. Sometimes just getting on the floor and putting up some shots is all you need to keep the touch alive, and keep active. Additionally, ATU Rec will host a weekly watch party on Sunday evenings for the early NBA games, to start the week off right ;)

ATU Rec - Campus Chairpeople

Each ATU campus must elect one person to be chairperson for the academic year. The chairpeople then coordinate for the end of year tournament.

Elections must be completed by October 15th each academic year to ensure the league goes ahead.
